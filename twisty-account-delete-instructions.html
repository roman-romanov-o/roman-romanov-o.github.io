If you want to delete your account and personal information, that is stored in app, you should follow this steps: You need to be logged in in application recently to delete account. If you are not logged in, recently, on step of account delete application will redirect you to login page.

  • Step 1: Open sidebar menu of application

  • Step 2: Click on "Settings" button

  • Step 3: Click on "Delete account" button

  • Step 4: Confirm deletion of account
